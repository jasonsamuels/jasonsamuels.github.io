### Federal agencies

*  [Executive Order 13583](https://drive.google.com/file/d/0BxzeR4LBHzkJSFZoUVdCRzNmTGs/view?usp=sharing)

This executive order, issued by the Obama White House in August 2011, mandated that all executive departments and agencies implement an agency-specific Diversity and Inclusion Strategic Plan. The proliferation of government agency diversity and inclusion plans listed below appears to have stemmed from this action.
